\begin{abstract}
%
  This note summarises the simulation results on three background sources, local beam-gas and beam-halo (IR7) and for the first time off-momentum (IR3) leakage. They all produce particle showers at the to the experimental areas, this was simulated for the ATLAS IP. Even though shower results are shown only for the IR1 FLUKA geometry, this note contains an leakage factors from IR3 and IR7 also for CMS. The most dominant sources, halo and beam-gas, have been systematically studied for LHC Run~I and Run~II cases, and several HL--LHC scenarios. We indicate rates in all these scenarios.
  
\end{abstract}
